Welcome to Cotton & Co

Cotton & Co is a Raw Cotton sourcing company specializing in indenting of Cotton from CIS, India, Pakistan, African, US and other origin. Our policy is to assure good quality & prompt shipment and settle any claim ASAP. Our mission is to achieve sound performance in this sector so that we can be the leading sourcing agent in Bangladesh.
